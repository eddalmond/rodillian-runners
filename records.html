Club Records

Track our members' achievements across classic race distances

We maintain club records for various age groups across classic race distances including 5K, 10K, half marathon, and marathon.

Qualifying Times

Records are accepted from UK Athletics licensed road races or parkrun events. Times must be officially recorded and verified.

Updating Records

To update club records, please contact Jon Vaile with your official race results including race name, date, distance, and finishing time.

Note

These charts have been compiled from recent years. There may be gaps in the records where we don't have complete data. If you have historical results that should be included, please get in touch.

Have Questions?

Contact Jon Vaile to discuss club records or to submit your achievements.
