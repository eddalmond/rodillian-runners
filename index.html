About Our Club

Who We Are

Rodillian Runners is a community-based running club dedicated to promoting fitness, friendship, and fun through running. We cater for all paces and abilities, from complete beginners to experienced marathon runners. Our ethos is simple: no runner is ever left behind.

What We Do

We organise weekly group runs, training sessions, social events, and enter local races throughout the year. Our experienced run leaders ensure everyone feels welcome and supported on their running journey.

Why Join

Running with others keeps you motivated, makes training more enjoyable, and helps you improve faster. Our club is inclusive, non-competitive, and focused on helping each other achieve our goals.
